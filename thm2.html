TryHackMe – Junior Penetration Tester Path

I wanted to go deeper into cybersecurity and finish what i started with the Beginner Path. So i enrolled in the Jr Pentester Path on TryHackMe.

This training was exactly what i was looking for(if not more). It got hands-on practical and immersive exercices . It gave me the essential foundations to start tackling my first pentesting challenges on both TryHackMe and HackTheBox. The learning curve was steep, but that made it even more addictive. I felt the adrenaline rush every time i cracked a machine or exploited a vulnerability.

Throughout the course, i covered the following topics such as:

  • Web application vulnerabilities
  • Password attacks and brute force techniques
  • SQL injection
  • Active Directory fundamentals
  • Database exploitation
  • Privilege escalation
  • And much more...

What made this experience so special was how it helped me to step into the mindeset of a real web penetration tester. It wasn't always easy to manage because a day i only 24h hours so i often find myself sacrificing sleep to practice more. I think that my strength was my motivation, it kept me going but my biggest challenge was patience i always wanted to go faster and go deeper, maybe sometimes too fast?

Anyway, in the end, i feel like it paid off. I actually ended in the top 5% of the global ranking of TryHackMe. It's not an elite score, but to me it represents my offcial entry point into the world of ethical hacking. I'm jumping into the rabbit hole and it's just the beginning.
